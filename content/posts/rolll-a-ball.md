<h1 text-align: center> Roll a ball: a unity project for android </h1>
For one of my class, I had to implement a "roll a ball" game: a 3D game destined for Androids where the controller can move around a ball by making it roll. 
I decided to implement it as a maze on the theme of space that the user has to solve while collecting stars collectibles.
